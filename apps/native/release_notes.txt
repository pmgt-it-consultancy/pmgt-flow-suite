## What's New

- Fixed the Takeout Orders screen freezing when tapping "Ready for Pickup" or other action buttons in quick succession — rapid taps no longer wedge the app's touch handling
- Each takeout order card now has an explicit "View Details" / "View Receipt" button for opening the order detail and reprint screen
- Voided takeout orders can now be opened from the list (previously had no tap affordance to reach the detail modal)
