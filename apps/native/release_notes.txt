## What's New

- Fix duplicate transactions — prevents double-tap from creating duplicate orders or processing payments twice
- Backend idempotency — retrying order submission or payment no longer creates duplicates
- Loading guards on all action buttons — buttons disable while processing to prevent accidental double-taps across takeout and dine-in flows

## Notes

- Affects both takeout and dine-in order flows
- Existing orders are unaffected (backward compatible)
