## What's New

- Add a new Day Closing screen for managers with daily sales, VAT, discount, and payment summaries
- Print Z-Reports directly from the POS app using the configured receipt printer
- Batch reprint receipts for selected paid orders from a chosen business date
- Support backdated day-closing review for late reconciliation

## Fixes

- Paid takeout orders now move to `Preparing` automatically after checkout
- Day-closing actions are now logged for better audit visibility

## Notes

- Day Closing is available from the home screen for users with the appropriate permission
- Batch receipt reprint requires a connected and configured receipt printer
