## What's New

- Native-thread touch handling — all buttons now use react-native-gesture-handler Pressable for instant press feedback even during heavy operations (printing, syncing)
- Android ripple effect on all interactive buttons for clear material tap feedback
- Memoized list items (cart, products, tables, orders) to prevent unnecessary re-renders
- Virtualized active orders list on home screen for smoother scrolling during busy service
- Hoisted currency formatter to module scope — eliminates repeated Intl.NumberFormat creation per list item
- Stabilized FlatList render callbacks with useCallback across all screens
