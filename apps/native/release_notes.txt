## What's New

- Takeout running bill — add items to advance takeout orders and send to kitchen multiple times before settling payment, just like dine-in
- Smart kitchen buttons on order screen — "Send to Kitchen" when new items exist, "Reprint Kitchen Receipt" when all items already sent
- Kitchen receipt prints only new items — subsequent sends print just the newly added items, not the full order
- Two-button takeout card — advance orders show "Add Items" and "Take Payment" side by side in the queue
- Checkout screen simplified — kitchen actions moved to the order screen where they belong
