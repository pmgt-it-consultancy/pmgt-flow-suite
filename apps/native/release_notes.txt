## What's New

- Open price products — products can now be configured as "open price" where the cashier enters the price at order time, with min/max validation
- Product card display — open price products show "Enter Price" label instead of a fixed price
- Price input in order flow — AddItemModal and ModifierSelectionModal now support inline price entry for open price products
- Web admin — new Open Price toggle in product form with min/max price configuration
- Takeout support — open price products work in both dine-in and takeout order flows

## Notes

- To use this feature, toggle "Open Price" when creating or editing a product in the web admin and set the min/max price range
