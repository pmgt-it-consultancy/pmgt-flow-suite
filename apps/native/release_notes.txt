## What's New

- Day Closing now correctly captures late-night shifts. Stores that close after midnight (e.g. 5 PM → 1 AM) get a single continuous Z-Report instead of splitting the shift across two dates.
- Custom Range time picker now defaults to the store's configured opening and closing hours for the selected weekday, and shows a small "(next day)" label under the end time when the range crosses midnight.
- Printed Z-Report now appends "(next day)" to the time range line whenever the custom range wraps past midnight, so the printout is self-explanatory.

## Notes

- Requires the store's weekly schedule to be configured in the admin panel for the automatic "Full Day" late-night behavior. Stores without a schedule still default to midnight-to-midnight cutoffs.
