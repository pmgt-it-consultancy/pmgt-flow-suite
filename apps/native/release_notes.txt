## What's New

- Draft takeout orders — customers can step aside while deciding, and their order is saved as a draft that any staff member can resume later
- Auto-generated customer labels — drafts are labeled "Customer #1", "Customer #2", etc. for easy identification, editable to real names
- Drafts section on takeout list — amber highlighted section at the top shows all parked orders with resume and discard actions
- End-of-day draft cleanup — leftover drafts are automatically cleaned up when the daily report is generated
- Receipt preview fix — cashless payments now show the actual payment type (GCash, Maya, etc.) and reference number instead of generic "Card" label

## Notes

- Staff tap "New Order" to create a draft, add items, then go back to park it — no extra save step needed
- Drafts are stored in the backend and visible across all devices
- The "Proceed to Payment" button submits the draft and assigns an order number at that point
