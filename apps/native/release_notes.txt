## What's New

- Cash drawer support via receipt printer's DK port (RJ-11/RJ-12 interface)
- Auto-open cash drawer after payment completion (configurable in Printer Settings)
- Manual "Open Cash Drawer" button in Printer Settings for quick access

## Notes

- Cash drawer requires a receipt printer with a DK (Drawer Kick) port connected to the cash drawer
- Enable the "Cash Drawer" toggle in Printer Settings to activate auto-open on checkout
